# Content prompts

Use these prompts after the scaffold and validator are in place.

## Vocabulary

Generate B1-B2 vocabulary items for Korean-speaking learners. Keep translations natural and examples short. Use the schema in the planning document.

## Grammar

Generate focused grammar topics with one point per lesson. Include fill-in questions, multiple-choice questions, and Korean explanations.

## Reading

Generate short reading passages with tokenized Spanish text and Korean glosses for each content token.

## Situations

Generate roleplay scenes with dialogues, key phrases, and shadowing lines.
